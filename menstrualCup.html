Menstrual Cup

How to Use

To use a menstrual cup, wash your hands and fold the cup using a C-fold or punch-down method. Insert it gently into the vagina, aiming toward your tailbone, and let it open fully to form a seal. You can wear it for 4 to 12 hours depending on your flow. When removing, pinch the base to release the seal, pull it out gently, and empty the contents. Rinse the cup with water or mild soap before reinserting. Between cycles, sterilize the cup by boiling it or using sterilizing tablets.

Advantages

  • Eco-friendly and reusable
  • Can be worn up to 12 hours
  • Long-term cost savings

Considerations

  • ⚠️ Learning curve for insertion/removal
  • ⚠️ Needs cleaning and sterilization
