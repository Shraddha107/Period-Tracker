Sanitary Pads

How to Use

To use a sanitary pad, first unwrap it from its packaging. Peel off the adhesive backing and stick it to the center of your underwear, making sure it's aligned with where menstrual flow occurs. If the pad has wings, fold them over the edges of your underwear to secure it. Change the pad every 3 to 6 hours or whenever it feels saturated to prevent leaks and maintain hygiene. After use, wrap the pad and dispose of it in a waste bin—never flush it. Wash your hands before and after handling for cleanliness, and consider carrying extras while on the go during your cycle.
