Panty Liners

How to Use

Panty liners are designed for light daily protection and are incredibly simple to use. Start by peeling off the adhesive backing and sticking the liner directly to the center of your underwear, making sure it’s flat and secure. They’re ideal for managing vaginal discharge, spotting, or providing backup when using tampons or menstrual cups. You can wear them throughout the day to stay fresh, but it's best to change them every few hours to maintain hygiene. Unlike pads, panty liners are thinner and more breathable, offering discreet comfort for light flow. After use, simply wrap and discard them in a waste bin—never flush them. Always wash your hands before and after use for cleanliness.

Advantages

  • Thin and breathable
  • Great for daily freshness
  • Easy to use and dispose

Considerations

  • ⚠️ Not suitable for heavy flow
  • ⚠️ May cause irritation with prolonged use
