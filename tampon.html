How to Use

To use a tampon, start by washing your hands. Choose the right absorbency for your flow and unwrap the tampon. You can insert it using the applicator or your fingers—gently push it into the vagina until it feels comfortable, aiming slightly upward toward the lower back. The string should hang outside for easy removal. Tampons should be changed every 4 to 8 hours and never left in for more than 8 hours to reduce the risk of Toxic Shock Syndrome (TSS). Dispose of used tampons in a bin, not the toilet, and always wash your hands before and after handling.
